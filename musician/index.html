Background

Background

Classically trained guitarist with a focus on several genres including classical, jazz, blues, folk and classic rock. I studied classical guitar at West Chester university and jazz guitar with E. Shawn Qaissaunee from Wilmington DE.

Member of the Wilmington Classical Guitar Society and the Lehigh Valley Classical Guitar Society.

Influences: Earl Klugh, Lee Ritenour, Wes Montgomery, Jeff Golub, Jimmy Page, Steve Howe, Bach, Beethoven, John Williams , , , just to name a few.
